I am a Research Scientist at Seed-LLM-Model, [ByteDance USA](https://www.bytedance.com/en), where I work on developing efficient and scalable LLMs. I received my PhD from Monash University, under the supervision of Prof. [Bohan Zhuang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DFuDBBwAAAAJ&hl=en) and Prof. [Jianfei Cai](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=N6czCoUAAAAJ&hl=en). I am a member of [ZIP Lab](https://ziplab.github.io/). Prior to my Ph.D., I completed my master's degree at South China University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. [Mingkui Tan](https://tanmingkui.github.io/) and Prof. [Qingyao Wu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=n6e_2IgAAAAJ&hl=en).
